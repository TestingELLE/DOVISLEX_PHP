2 Bedroom/2 Bath in Luxury Bldg. near Medical Center/Museum District/Rice U/Dwtn

2-bedroom apartment in luxury building: Super-safe, modern, comfortable at Hermann Park and next to the Texas Medical Center and surrounding areas of Mid-town, the Museum District and Rice University. Many of our guests have either worked at the medical center, are patients here for treatments, initial or follow-up visits or are moving to Houston and need a place while looking for their new home. We understand and have experience with these situations and offer discounts for longer stays - please inquire! **** Covid-19 Ready: We do a thorough cleaning upon each guests arrival and departure to make sure the apartment is safe for all guests. Additionally we are providing initial sets of face masks and disposable gloves (latex free) for your use while traveling outside. Please remember to follow safe practices - often washing hands, practice social distancing, using delivery and curbside pick-up where possible and other CDC recommended suggestions. The building has a 24-hour concierge to make deliveries easy. Apartment details: There are two bedroom suites, each with its own bath. Kitchen with stainless steal appliances and granite counter tops, Dining area and Living Room overlooking the Park. You also have a fast WiFi/Internet Connection, AC which are all located in a very safe building with 24- hr. concierge, gated garage, gym and pool. Right across the street from Hermann Park located in the heart of the Texas Medical Center/Museum District and next to Downtown. Walking distance to the Medical Center with free shuttle bus available weekdays (many medical professionals live here and this building is often chosen by patients for its proximity to the hospitals). Also, a 10 min. drive to the many great restaurants in Midtown or Downtown. ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ Luxury building full of amenities in a great location by Medical Center, Hermann Park and Museum district also close to all major highways. Apartment has Master bedroom and guest room suites, each with their own bathroom and full wall closet. Our apartment offers an open floor plan featuring the kitchen (stainless steel appliances and granite counter tops), living room and dining area that end in a window wall with wonderful views of the pool and golf course. Included are utilities (WiFi, A/C, Heat, Water, Electric*) and you will have access to all building amenities, your own parking space and in unit washer and dryer. The building features, a resort style pool with hot tub and cabana lounging beds, a full size sports gym, club house, garage attendants and 24-hr concierge. This is a very safe building. Located at the Texas Medical Center, and the Museum District. Visit Hermann Park for the Houston Zoo, Miller Outdoor Theater, play a round at the 18 hole Golf Course or walk/ jog along its many paths and trails. All across the street! Or visit the Museum district for new exhibits and film productions. And just down the road, the Mid-town area offers nightlife and great dinning options. The Texas Medical Center is located 5 mins. away across Hermann Park and we are located on the free loop shuttle between the different Medical Center buildings. We are located near Route 288 for convenient access to all major Highways (Rt. 59 or 610) that can take you quickly to work or to explore Houston. Downtown with its sports arenas are 15 mins. away to the north or go shopping at the Galleria 25 mins. away. Two week minimum. (Except for the Summer months of June, July, or August when it is a 1 month minimum. And OTC when it is a 6 day minimum. Please inquire about Summer or OTC rates, fees and min.) *Internet is free. Utilities are included for free, up to the following amounts: Nov-Apr: $100.00 and May-Oct: $180 (this includes: heat/ water/ electric/ a/c) **Place is ideal for two guests with occasional visitors staying a few days during they stay (added charge over two guests) *** We love pets; but due to allergies can not accept pets at this time.
